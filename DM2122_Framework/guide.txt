====::Controls::====

::Keyboard::
W - Move forwards
S - Move backwards
A - Turn left  / Look left
D - Turn right / Look right

R - Look down
F - Look up
Q - Look right
E - Look left

::Mouse::
Left click - Slash in game / select in menu
Right click - Shoot in game

====::Objective::====

::Scene01::
Get a key to unlock door to proceed
While evading / eliminating enemies
Survive

::Scene02::
Solve the puzzles to proceed

::Scene03::
Solve puzzles
Eliminate enemies
Hop onto helicopter
